Description

Ciater Spa Resort is located in the legendary valley of Mount Tangkuban Perahu at an altitude of 1,050m above sea level with the benefits of natural hot springs and surrounded by green tea plantations make Ciater Spa Resort as an ideal place for a vacation with family and relatives as well as a place for gathering, meeting and outing. Ciater Spa Resort features superior wellness spa which is a blend of modern technology with hot water source for health benefits, handled by medical experts and physiotherapy, so that guests who come can take a vacation while restoring health and fitness.

Route

Facilities

Ciater Spa Resort not only make hot water as a menu dish. He is an integrated tourism area. There are also available outbound rides, rafting, paintball, flyng fox, horse riding, and so forth. Also, Ciater Spa Resort has a hotel and convention room.

Road Access

The Ciater Spa Resort gate is just a few meters away from the gates of the hotel's mosque: The Grand Mosque of As-Saadah. But the location of the resort itself is a little deep inside. As the name implies, he is a spa resort. Or if you want simplified: hot water bath. There is a heated swimming pool (Kiara Pool) and VIP bathroom for hot showers. Hot water is not artificial hot water, but natural hot water, which is a typical tourist area in Ciater, Subang Regency.
