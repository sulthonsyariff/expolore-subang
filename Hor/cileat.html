Description

Curug Cileat is the highest waterfall located in Subang Regency, this place there are 5 pieces of different waterfalls and can be found during the journey. Curug Cileat has a height of Â± 100 m and located in the area of Mount Canggah. Because of its location which is at the foot of the mountain, then to be able to meet this waterfall you have to prepare a little power for hiking to the foot of Mount Canggah. Although it is a bit difficult to reach this waterfall because of the extreme access road, but it will pay off with beautiful natural scenery and can see some waterfall opening before arriving at Curug Cileat.

Route

Facilities

There are no facilities in curug cileat, because the distance is very far from the settlement so the atmosphere is still beautiful and no buildings.

Route

Road Access

For those who use public transportation from the city terminal Subang Elf vehicle with the route Subang - Tanjung Siang. Get off at the dorms, then take a motorcycle taxi to Cibago Village. Then from the last village, Cibago Village, the journey continues on foot through a rocky path about 5 km to arrive at the location of the waterfall.
