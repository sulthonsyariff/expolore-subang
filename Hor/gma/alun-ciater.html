Description

Pamoyanan Hill is a familiar tour, once this place has also been laid out including already built MCK facilities. But then dormant because no one manage. Currently with a stretch of tourism in Subang this place will be re-managed by the local Karang Taruna under the leadership of Kang Yayat with the name of Bukit Alam Endah Pamoyanan.

    Road access

    Access to Pamoyanan hill can be reached through the pineapple of Jalancagak to the east. Then after the Tanjungsiang gas station and before the market Tanjungsiang drive to the village Kawungluwuk. The gateway to Kawungluwuk village from the west is on the right side of the road. Estimated trip from Subang city to Kawungluwuk village takes approximately 1 hour journey.
